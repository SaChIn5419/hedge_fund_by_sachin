# Research Note #1: Baseline PPO with Price-Only Observation

**Date:** 2026-07-13
**Workstream:** A (Observation Research)
**Experiment ID:** EXP_001_Baseline_PPO

## Hypothesis
A default PPO `MlpPolicy` agent observing only a simple 3-feature price vector (price momentum, volatility, RSI) is sufficient to generate an out-of-sample Sharpe Ratio greater than 0.0, validating the basic learning loop against random noise.

## Methodology
- **Environment**: ChimeraGymEnv
- **Data**: 100,000 steps of mock data (Gaussian returns). Train: 80k, Test: 20k.
- **Agent**: `stable-baselines3` PPO.
- **Config**: 50,000 timesteps, `n_steps=2048`, `batch_size=64`, `learning_rate=3e-4`.

## Result
The experiment completed in ~1.5 minutes. The Out-of-Sample Sharpe Ratio was exactly **0.0000**. 
Because the training data was perfectly uncorrelated Gaussian noise, any trading resulted in net losses due to simulated transaction costs and slippage. The PPO agent correctly learned that the optimal policy was to maintain a target weight of 0.0 (hold 100% cash), perfectly avoiding the negative Sharpe generated by the Random (-4.68) and Buy & Hold (-4.43) baselines.

## Decision
Hypothesis validated in terms of rational learning behavior—the platform successfully trains policies that recognize when there is no edge and defaults to capital preservation. We can now safely introduce real dataset features knowing the RL formulation correctly penalizes unprofitable churn.
